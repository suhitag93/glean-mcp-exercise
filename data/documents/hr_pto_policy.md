# Paid Time Off (PTO) Policy

**Document ID:** hr-pto-policy-v3  
**Category:** Human Resources  
**Last Updated:** 2025-01-15  
**Owner:** People Operations

## Overview

Acme Corp offers a flexible PTO policy to support employee well-being and work-life balance. All full-time employees accrue PTO from their first day of employment.

## Accrual Rates

| Employment Status | PTO Accrual |
|---|---|
| Years 0–2 | 15 days per year (1.25 days/month) |
| Years 2–5 | 20 days per year (1.67 days/month) |
| Years 5+ | 25 days per year (2.08 days/month) |

Part-time employees accrue PTO on a prorated basis based on scheduled hours.

## Rollover Policy

Employees may roll over up to **10 days** of unused PTO into the next calendar year. Any balance above 10 days is forfeited on December 31st unless a manager-approved exception is granted.

## How to Request PTO

1. Submit a request in **Workday** at least 2 business days in advance for absences up to 3 days.
2. For absences of 4+ days, provide at least 2 weeks notice when possible.
3. Your manager must approve all PTO requests. Requests are denied if they conflict with critical business deadlines or leave the team understaffed.
4. Once approved, PTO automatically syncs to your Google Calendar.

## Sick Leave

Sick leave is separate from PTO. Employees receive **10 sick days** per calendar year that do not roll over. Sick days are not subject to advance notice requirements.

## Holidays

Acme Corp observes **11 federal holidays** plus 2 floating holidays that employees may use at any time. Floating holidays expire at year-end.

## Bereavement Leave

Employees receive up to 5 consecutive paid days for the loss of an immediate family member (spouse, child, parent, sibling). 3 days are provided for extended family (grandparent, aunt/uncle, in-laws).
