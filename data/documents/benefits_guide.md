## Overview

Acme Corp offers a comprehensive benefits package to all full-time employees. Benefits take effect on the first of the month following your start date, with a 30-day enrollment window.

## Health Insurance

### Medical

Acme Corp offers three medical plan tiers through Aetna:

| Plan | Monthly Premium (Employee) | Deductible | Out-of-Pocket Max |
|---|---|---|---|
| Bronze HSA | $0 | $1,500 individual / $3,000 family | $4,000 / $8,000 |
| Silver PPO | $85 | $500 individual / $1,000 family | $2,500 / $5,000 |
| Gold PPO | $210 | $250 individual / $500 family | $1,500 / $3,000 |

Acme Corp covers 100% of the employee premium for Bronze HSA and contributes $125/month to employee HSA accounts.

### Dental

Dental coverage through Delta Dental PPO:
- Preventive care: 100% covered (no waiting period)
- Basic restorative: 80% covered after 6-month waiting period
- Major restorative/orthodontia: 50% covered after 12-month waiting period
- Annual maximum benefit: $2,000 per person

### Vision

VSP Vision coverage:
- Annual eye exam: $10 copay
- Frames: up to $150 allowance per year
- Contact lenses: up to $150 allowance per year

## Retirement

### 401(k) Plan

- Traditional 401(k) and Roth 401(k) available through Fidelity
- **Company match**: 50% of contributions up to 6% of salary (i.e., max 3% match)
- Vesting schedule: 25% per year, fully vested after 4 years
- 2025 contribution limit: $23,500 (IRS limit); $31,000 if age 50+
- Enrollment open year-round; changes take effect the following pay period

## Additional Benefits

### Parental Leave

- **Primary caregiver**: 16 weeks fully paid leave
- **Secondary caregiver**: 6 weeks fully paid leave
- Applies to birth, adoption, or foster placement
- Must be taken within 12 months of the qualifying event

### Learning & Development

- **$2,000 annual L&D budget** per employee for courses, conferences, books, or certifications
- Submit reimbursement requests in Workday with receipt and manager approval
- Unused budget does not roll over

### Mental Health

- EAP (Employee Assistance Program) through Lyra Health: up to 16 free therapy sessions per year
- Calm app subscription provided at no cost

### Commuter Benefits

- Pre-tax transit/parking benefits up to IRS limit ($315/month in 2025)
- Managed through Commuter Benefits portal in Workday

### Equity

- RSUs (Restricted Stock Units) granted to eligible employees based on role and performance
- Standard vesting: 25% cliff at 1 year, then monthly vesting over 3 additional years
- Equity details are provided in your offer letter and managed in Carta

## Open Enrollment

Annual open enrollment runs each **November** for coverage starting January 1. Life events (marriage, birth, loss of other coverage) trigger a 30-day special enrollment window.
