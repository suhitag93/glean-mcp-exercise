# Remote and Hybrid Work Policy

**Document ID:** hr-remote-work-v2  
**Category:** Human Resources  
**Last Updated:** 2025-01-10  
**Owner:** People Operations

## Overview

Acme Corp operates as a **hybrid-first** company. Most roles can be performed remotely, but we value in-person collaboration and maintain office hubs in San Francisco, New York, and London.

## Work Arrangements

### Hybrid (Default)

Employees in the SF, NY, or London metro areas are expected to work from the office at least **2 days per week**. Specific days are set at the team level by your manager. The default for most engineering and product teams is Tuesday and Thursday.

### Fully Remote

Employees hired as fully remote may work from any location within their approved country of employment. Relocation to a new country requires advance approval from People Ops and Legal (tax/legal implications).

### Fully On-site

Certain roles (IT support, facilities, lab operations) require full-time on-site presence. These are specified in the job description.

## Home Office Stipend

All remote and hybrid employees receive a **$1,500 one-time home office stipend** upon joining and a **$500 annual refresh stipend** for equipment, ergonomic accessories, or office supplies.

To claim:
1. Purchase items and keep receipts
2. Submit in Workday under "Expense Report > Home Office"
3. Reimbursement is processed within 2 pay cycles

## Core Hours

Regardless of location, all employees are expected to be available during **core hours: 10 AM – 3 PM in their local time zone**. Meetings should be scheduled within this window to the extent possible. Respect for time zones is expected; no mandatory meetings before 8 AM or after 6 PM local time without prior consent.

## International Travel

Employees wishing to work from a country other than their country of employment for more than **10 consecutive days** must notify People Ops and Legal. Working abroad can create tax, legal, and visa complications. Acme Corp currently has approved "work abroad" programs with 12 countries including Canada, UK, Germany, France, Australia, Japan, and Singapore.

## Equipment and Security

- Company-issued laptops must be used for all work. Personal devices are not permitted for accessing production systems.
- Always use the corporate VPN when accessing internal systems from outside the office.
- Do not work from public Wi-Fi without VPN active.

## Expense Reimbursement for Travel

When traveling to an Acme Corp office, travel and accommodation are reimbursable per the Travel Expense Policy (see doc: hr-travel-expense-policy). Submit expenses within 30 days of travel via Workday.

## Coworking Spaces

Acme Corp has WeWork All Access memberships available to remote employees who prefer a coworking environment. Request access through the IT Helpdesk portal. Available in 500+ locations worldwide.

## Performance and Accountability

Remote work is a privilege, not an entitlement. Performance expectations are identical regardless of work location. If performance issues arise that are related to remote work challenges, your manager will work with you on a resolution before considering changes to your work arrangement.
